Kayaking
  • Kayaking in Dandeli is mostly conducted in Supa Reservoir or River Kali.

  • Dandeli is the only place in south India where one can attempt to learn kayaking to advanced levels.

  • It is one of the main Dandeli activities. Kayaking is an activity with the use of a kayak for moving across water.

  • Kayaking can be done in vast expanses of still waters or in rapids, the latter being more challenging.

  • For this one must be a strong swimmer and have sufficient kayaking experience.

  • Before kayaking in the Supa Dam region, instructors and guides give visitors a demo.

  • They provide introductory sessions on the basic techniques about strokes, basic control and the balance of the kayak.
